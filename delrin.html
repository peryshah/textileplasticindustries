What is Delrin?

Delrin, also known as Polyoxymethylene (POM), is a high-performance thermoplastic renowned for its excellent mechanical properties, including:

  • High Strength and Stiffness: Ideal for applications requiring durability and precision.
  • Low Friction and Wear: Perfect for moving parts, reducing energy consumption and maintenance.
  • Dimensional Stability: Maintains its shape and size under varying temperatures and humidity.
  • Chemical Resistance: Resistant to solvents, fuels, and many chemicals, making it suitable for harsh environments.

Delrin is widely used in industries such as automotive, electronics, consumer goods, and more, making it a reliable choice for demanding applications.

Applications of Delrin

  • Automotive: Gears, bearings, and bushings for high-performance and low-friction applications.
  • Electronics: Insulators, connectors, and housings for electrical components.
  • Consumer Goods: Zippers, rollers, and handles for durable and smooth operation.
  • Industrial Equipment: Seals, rollers, and conveyor components for wear-resistant applications.
