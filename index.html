COMPANY PROFILE

We TEXTILE PLASTIC INDUSTRIES are certified manufacturers and exporters of a wide range of parts, components & accessories of Industrial conveyor systems. We offer conveyor system parts, components & accessories and have been successfully catering to various industries such as light engineering, food processing, electrical and electronics, marine, textile and automobile.
