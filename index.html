COMPANY PROFILE

We TEXTILE PLASTIC INDUSTRIES are certified manufacturers and exporters of a wide range of parts, components & accessories of Industrial conveyor systems. The array offering includes UHMWPE Chain Guide, Wear strips, Corner tracks, Drive Sprockets, Star Plate, and UHMWPE Drive Wheel. To maintain the high quality standards we make use of high grade raw material such as NYLON, UHMWPE that are procured from the markets of India, Germany and Italy. We offer conveyor system parts, components & accessories and have been successfully catering to various industries such as light engineering, food processing, electrical and electronics, marine, textile and automobile. Further, to meet the diverse requirements of our clients, we also offer customization of products, to meet the specific requirements of clients.
