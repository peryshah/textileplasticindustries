Our Operations

At Textile Plastic Industries, we take pride in our state-of-the-art in-house fabrication facility, equipped with advanced machinery and a highly skilled team. Our operations are designed to deliver precision-engineered plastic components tailored to meet your specific requirements.

Our Machinery

  • Circular Saw: For precise cutting of industrial plastics.
  • Planner: For smooth and accurate surface finishing.
  • VMC (Vertical Machining Center): For high-precision machining of complex parts.
  • CNC Machines: For automated and accurate fabrication.
  • Lathe Machines: For turning and shaping plastic materials.

Our Processes

We follow a streamlined process to ensure the highest quality standards:

  1. Material Selection: Choosing the right industrial plastic (UHMWPE, HDPE, PP, Teflon, etc.) for your application.
  2. Cutting & Shaping: Using advanced machinery to cut and shape the material as per design specifications.
  3. Machining: Precision machining to achieve tight tolerances and smooth finishes.
  4. Quality Inspection: Rigorous quality checks to ensure every component meets industry standards.
  5. Packaging & Delivery: Safe packaging and timely delivery of finished products.
